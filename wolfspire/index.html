A prosperous port city on the eastern coast. Exotic goods, weathered sailors, and the wreck-strewn sea await.
🌿
Wolfhaven
Druidic settlement — North
A sacred settlement of druids and mages. Ancient wisdom, nature magic, and the shadow of the Ashwood beyond.
🌲
Ironwood
Elven warrior settlement — West
A fortress of living ironwood where elven blades, rangers, and battle-mages stand watch against the blight below.
🏇
Windmane

A centaur settlement on the open steppe. Swift hunters, open sky, and canyons that swallow the unwary.

Rocky highlands crawling with goblins, orcs, ogres, and hill giants. No magic, no corruption — just raw, crushing force.
