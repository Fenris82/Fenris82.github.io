Wolfspire
Early Access

A free, browser-based RPG dungeon crawler with no paywalls, no energy timers, and no compromises. Build your character across six attributes, then set out from Greatwolf City into a growing world of towns, dungeons, and secrets.

Explore over 20 dungeons — volcanic depths, crystal caverns, frozen wastes, shadow realms, and more — each with their own enemies, unique loot, and boss encounters every ten floors. Level up, unlock skills and spells, forge your gear to ever-greater power, and equip across a full inventory system.

When you're not fighting: fish at Sunport Harbor, play Wolf Cards, Rune Code, Wolf Dice, or Wolf Tower in the Game Room, or piece together the world's story through an in-game journal. Full screen reader and VoiceOver support built in from day one. Runs in any browser, on any device, no download required.

Wolfspire is actively in development — more dungeons, lore, music, sound effects, and ambience are on the way.

Who We Are

Built Different

Greatwolf Games is an indie studio built on one belief — great games should be for everyone. We make accessible, ethical games that respect players.

♿
Accessibility First

Full screen reader support built in from day one — not bolted on as an afterthought. Every button, every action, every piece of content is designed to work for everyone.

🚫
No Dark Patterns

No energy timers. No pay-to-win. No gacha. No artificial friction. We believe friction in a game should serve the player experience — never exploit it.

🆓
Free to Play, Always

Every game we make is free. If you want to support us down the line there will be optional ways to do that — but you will never need to pay to enjoy the full experience.

🌍
Play Anywhere

All our games run in your browser. iPhone, Android, Mac, Windows, any device with a browser. No downloads, no installs, no app store gatekeeping.
